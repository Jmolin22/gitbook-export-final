# Welcome to: Cybersecurity Awareness & Best Practices

### Welcome

This site is a public, educational resource focused on practical cybersecurity best practices, real‑world scenarios, and plain‑language explanations of common security risks.

It is designed to help learners, staff, and community members better understand how to protect themselves and their organizations in an increasingly digital world.

**Who This Is For**

This resource is intended for:

* Students and early‑career learners exploring cybersecurity fundamentals
* Employees and staff looking to improve everyday security habits
* Educators and trainers seeking reusable teaching material
* Organizations and partners interested in awareness‑level security guidance

No advanced technical background is required.

&#x20;

**What You’ll Find Here**

You’ll find content focused on:

* Common cyber threats (phishing, malware, account compromise)
* Everyday best practices (passwords, MFA, email safety)
* Real‑world scenarios and “what to do next” guidance
* Clear definitions of security terms and concepts

This site focuses on awareness and education, not internal or sensitive operational procedures.

&#x20;

**How to Use This Resource**

* Browse topics by section from the navigation menu
* Use scenarios to understand how attacks commonly unfold
* Share pages freely for training or awareness purposes
* Revisit regularly — content is updated as threats evolve

&#x20;

**What This Resource Is Not**

To maintain safety and clarity, this site does not include:

* Internal response playbooks
* Organization‑specific procedures
* Detailed attack instructions
* Confidential or sensitive operational details
